## Overview

DoseBar is a macOS menu bar application for medication reminders. Your privacy is important to us, and DoseBar is designed with a privacy-first approach.

## Data Collection

**DoseBar does not collect, transmit, or share any data.** Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No health or medication data leaves your device
- No analytics or tracking of any kind
- No network connections are made
- No third-party SDKs or frameworks are included
- No cookies or identifiers are used

## Data Storage

All medication names, dosages, and schedule data are stored **locally on your Mac** using macOS UserDefaults within the app's sandboxed container. This data never leaves your device.

## Notifications

DoseBar uses the macOS notification system to send medication reminders. Notification content is generated locally and is not transmitted to any server.
